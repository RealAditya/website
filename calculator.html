Electricity Bill Calculator







How does it work?

You pay a rate of 10 INR per unit for the first 100 units.

If the number of units is more than 100 but less than 200 then each unit is charged at INR 15.

Beyond 200 units each unit is charged at INR 20

If the number of units is more than 300 but less than 200 then each unit is charged at INR 25.
